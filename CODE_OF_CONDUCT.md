# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone.  
All participants are expected to uphold these standards in any project-related spaces, including GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and chats.

### Positive Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate of others  
- Use welcoming and inclusive language  
- Offer constructive feedback  
- Help others learn and grow  

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination  
- Personal attacks or offensive comments  
- Disruptive or harmful behavior to the community  
- Sharing private information without consent  

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainers:  
**Email:** security@woubleu.dev  

Reports will be handled **confidentially** and respectfully.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this code of conduct.  
Maintainers may:
- Warn or remove participants who violate the rules  
- Close issues or PRs that are abusive  
- Take any action necessary to maintain a safe and welcoming environment
